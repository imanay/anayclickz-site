About

Photography with a sense of place

This site is designed as a simple portfolio for the visual identity of @anay_clickz. It highlights atmospheric travel moments, natural landscapes, and wildlife details, while keeping the original Instagram posts easy to open and share.

Creative Direction

Clean and immersive

Large typography, dark presentation, responsive layout, and embedded Instagram posts that give visitors a direct path to the original work.

Environment

Outdoor atmosphere, mood, place, and storytelling rooted in the natural world.
